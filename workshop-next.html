Annual gathering of the work group 2022.

MedVis&BioVis Workshop 2022

  • LOCATION

    VRVis, Vienna

  • DATE

    21 September 2022

  • Organizers

    Katja Bühler, Anja Hennemuth, and Timo Ropinski

Registration and Get-together at VRVis (9:00)

Programme

Opening (9:30 – 9:45)

Visualisation

  • Bernhard Preim, OVGU: Metaphors in Medical Diagnosis, Treatment and Education.
  • Sarah Mittenentzwei, OVGU: Narrative Medical Visualization.
  • Helwig Hauser, Uni Bergen: Fjord-side Biomedical Visualization.

Coffee Break (11:00 – 11:30)

Presentation of VisGroup, TU Wien

  • Renata Raidou, TU Wien: Biomedical visuaulzation research at the VisGroup of TU Wien - An Overview

VR in Medicine

  • Jan Hombeck, Uni Jena: Evaluating Perceptional Tasks for Medicine in VR.
  • Mareen Allgaier, OVGU: Gamification of medical VR training applications.

Lunch Break (12:45 – 13:30)

Visual Computing for Diagnosis and Treatment Planning of the Cardiovascular System

  • Pepe Eulzer, Uni Jena: Map-Like Visualizations of the Cardiovascular System.
  • Chiara Manini, Charit&eaccute;: Multimodal mitral valve assessment for diagnosis and treatment planning.
  • Nina Krüger, Charité: Image processing for implantable device development and treatment support.

Coffee Break (14:45-15:15)

Learning-based Methods

  • Timo Ropinski, Uni Ulm: Learning-based Electron Microscopy Imaging.
  • Angelika Szengel and Dennis Jentsch, 1000shapes: Statistical shape modeling at 1000shapes.
  • Maria Wimmer, VRVis: Tackling Data Heterogeneity in Medical Image Analysis.

Closing (16:45 – 17:00)

to declare how many people of your work group will be attending, and if you would like contribute a talk (which would be great!). -->
