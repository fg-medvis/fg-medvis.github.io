Welcome to
Visual Computing in Biology and Medicine

A Work Group of the German Gesellschaft für Informatik e.V. (GI) and associated to the Deutsche Gesellschaft für Computer- und Roboterassistierte Chirurgie e.V. (CURAC).

Mission Statement

Expert Workshops

We initiate annual workshop meetings to foster exchange between academic developers - computer scientists, engineers and natural scientists - about image analysis, visualization, and interaction techniques in image-based diagnostics and therapy, computer-assisted therapy planning and about software tools related thereto.

Workshops

Awarding Excellence

We award excellent contributions in the field of medical and biological visualization. The Karl-Heinz Höhne Award is assigned biennially by a jury of academics and industry representatives.
The next competition takes place in spring 2020.
